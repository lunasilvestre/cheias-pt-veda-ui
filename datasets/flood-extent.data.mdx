## Rapid Flood Mapping

Within hours of major flooding, the Copernicus Emergency Management Service deploys satellite imagery analysts to map the extent of affected areas. These maps guide emergency response and recovery planning.

The EMSR864 activation covered 13 areas of interest across Portugal during the February 2026 storm sequence, mapping 89,199 hectares of inundation — 5,496 flood polygons dissolved from 14,747 original Sentinel-1 SAR delineations.
