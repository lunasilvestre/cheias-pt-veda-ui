## Rapid Flood Mapping

Within hours of major flooding, the Copernicus Emergency Management Service deploys satellite imagery analysts to map the extent of affected areas. These maps guide emergency response and recovery planning.

The EMSR864 activation covered 13 areas of interest across Portugal during the February 2026 storm sequence, mapping a total of 226,764 hectares of inundation — 14,747 individual flood polygons delineated from Sentinel-1 SAR.
