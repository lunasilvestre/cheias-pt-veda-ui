## Rapid Flood Mapping

Within hours of major flooding, the Copernicus Emergency Management Service deploys satellite imagery analysts to map the extent of affected areas. These maps guide emergency response and recovery planning.

The EMSR861 and EMSR864 activations covered 13 areas of interest across Portugal during the January-February 2026 storm sequence, mapping a total of 226,764 hectares of inundation.
