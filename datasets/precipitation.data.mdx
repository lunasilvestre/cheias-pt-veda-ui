## About This Dataset

Daily accumulated precipitation from the ERA5 reanalysis product. January 2026 delivered 222% of the 1991-2020 average rainfall across Portugal — the 2nd wettest January in the past 26 years.

Storm Kristin (Jan 28-30), Leonardo (Feb 3-7), and Marta (Feb 6-8) each brought 50-100+ mm of rain to already-saturated ground, triggering catastrophic flooding.
