## About This Dataset

Soil moisture is a critical precursor to flooding. When soils are already saturated and rain is forecasted, flood risk increases dramatically because rainfall flows overland directly into rivers rather than being absorbed.

Portugal's soils entered January 2026 already near saturation (0.42 m³/m³ average) after December delivered 222% of normal rainfall. This left the landscape primed for catastrophic flooding.
