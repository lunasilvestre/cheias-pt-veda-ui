## About This Dataset

Integrated Water Vapour Transport (IVT) measures the total amount of moisture flowing through the atmosphere. Values above 250 kg/m/s typically indicate an atmospheric river — a narrow corridor of concentrated moisture that can deliver extreme precipitation when it makes landfall.

Storm Kristin rode an atmospheric river that peaked above 700 kg/m/s as it struck Portugal on January 28, 2026, funnelling tropical moisture directly into the Iberian Peninsula.
