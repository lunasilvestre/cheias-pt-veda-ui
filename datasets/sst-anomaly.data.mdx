## About This Dataset

Sea surface temperatures off Portugal in late January 2026 were 2-3 C warmer than the 1991-2020 average. Warm ocean waters evaporate more moisture into the atmosphere, fueling stronger storms and heavier rainfall.

When Storm Kristin developed over these anomalously warm waters, the atmosphere carried approximately 7% more moisture than it would have in a pre-warming climate.
