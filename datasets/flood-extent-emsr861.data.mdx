## Storm Kristin — EMSR861

The Copernicus Emergency Management Service activated EMSR861 in response to Storm Kristin (Jan 28–30, 2026). Sentinel-1 SAR imagery was used to map 506 flood polygons across the Coimbra region, where the Mondego river breached its banks.
