## About This Dataset

The compound flood precondition index combines three drivers that must align for catastrophic flooding: warm sea surface temperatures (fuelling moisture), saturated soils (reducing infiltration), and atmospheric river activity (delivering rainfall).

By late January 2026, all three indicators were simultaneously at extreme levels across Portugal — a convergence that made the subsequent flooding nearly inevitable once Storm Kristin arrived.
