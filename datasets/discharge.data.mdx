## About This Dataset

River discharge measurements from the Portuguese national hydrological monitoring network (SNIRH) and the Global Flood Awareness System (GloFAS).

The Tejo river peaked at 6,775 m3/s on February 7, 2026 — exceeding its 2,000 m3/s design capacity by 238%. This was one of the highest discharges in modern records.
