## About This Dataset

Mean sea level pressure (MSLP) reveals the position and intensity of storm systems. Deep low-pressure centres — below 980 hPa — indicate powerful cyclones capable of driving extreme wind and rainfall.

During the Winter 2025-26 crisis, three successive storms (Kristin, Leonardo, Marta) tracked across the North Atlantic with central pressures below 985 hPa, each steering moisture-laden air directly onto Portugal.
